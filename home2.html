Our Story

Born from a Garage,
Raised in the Industrial District

What started as a 5-gallon homebrew setup in a rented garage has grown into Portland's most talked-about craft microbrewery. Founded by lifelong friends Marco and Elijah, Hops Haven is built on the belief that beer should be bold, honest, and never mass-produced.

Every recipe is developed in-house, every batch is hand-crafted, and every pour is served with pride. We don't cut corners � we condition, we taste, we refine, and then we share.

Our Process

The Grain-to-Glass Journey

Every batch begins with intention. From selecting the finest grains to the final pour � here's how we brew.

1

Milling & Mashing

We crack premium PNW malted barley and gently mash to extract fermentable sugars.

2

Boil & Hopping

The wort comes to a vigorous boil where we add our signature hop blends at precise intervals.

3

Fermentation

Our house yeast strains go to work, transforming wort into beer over 10-21 days.

4

Conditioning

Cold conditioning and carbonation perfect the flavor before it reaches your glass.
