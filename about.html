Our Story

A Garage, a Dream, and a Whole Lot of Hops

What started as a 5-gallon homebrew setup in a rented Portland garage quickly became an obsession. Founders Marco Villanueva and Elijah Park spent countless nights perfecting their recipes, studying water chemistry, and dreaming of a space where craft beer lovers could gather.

In the summer of 2024, that dream found a home in the city's Industrial District � a repurposed warehouse with exposed brick, raw timber beams, and enough room for a 10-barrel brewhouse. Today, Hops Haven stands as a testament to the idea that great beer is made with great intention.

"We don't make beer for everyone. We make beer for those who care about what's in their glass."

� Marco & Elijah, Co-Founders
